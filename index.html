About REHAF

REHAF is a process-first problem-solving framework. It exists to turn chaos into structure, reduce daily friction, and protect outcomes from human interference.

REHAF investigates before acting: observe → map the process → find breakpoints → surface root causes → enforce a safer workflow.

REHAF refuses outcomes without authority, micromanagement, delayed decisions, cosmetic “looks good” work, and babysitting.

This is REHAF.me: method + systems + lab. (REHAF.ae will later be the business offer.)
