About REHAF

REHAF exists to protect outcomes. Businesses fail at execution when decisions are delayed, processes are overridden, and accountability is unclear.

REHAF builds systems that survive human behavior: structure first, visibility second, tools last.

Boundary: If REHAF is accountable for results, REHAF needs authority to enforce the process. No outcome without control.
